Freak Chess Club welcomes friends, foes, or lovers of any skill level to sit and play. We hope that your experience will constantly change, but the game will always stay the same. Find us by listening for the sound of a blade of grass whistling in the wind. The devastated exclamation of a player who accidentally started taking the game too seriously. Or, check out our calendar page for more info. If you're feeling crazy you can even join our mailing list to stay in the loop about future events.
